WHAT
IS CALLED ART?

Art, also called (to distinguish it from other art forms) visual art, a visual object or experience consiously created through an expression of skill or imagination.

7 TYPES OF ART

The seven different art forms are Painting, sculpture, Literate, Architecture, Theater, Film and Music. However, back in the day, the seven different art forms were called the liberal arts, consisting of Grammar, Logic Rhetoric, Arithemetic, Geometry, Astonomy, and Musin.
